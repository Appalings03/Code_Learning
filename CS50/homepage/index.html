About Me

Hello! My name is Martin,I'm 23 years old and I'm passionate about rock climbing. I've been climbing for several years now, and I absolutely love the feeling of reaching the top after a challenging climb. My favorite place to climb is Fontainebleau near Paris in France, which I visit every year in July or August to explore its amazing boulders, with my friends.

In this website, you'll fin different pages I created to share my climbing experiences and to help others who are interested about the sport:

  • Sites page: This pages shows the differnets types of boulders I climb at FontaineBleau, with the names of each site.
  • Material Page: Here you can find climbing equipment that I recommend, perfect for any climbing excursion!
  • Inscription Page: This page is for those who want to join me on an adventure to Fontainebleau. It's a great opportunity to meet other climbers and enjoy outdoor bouldering.
  • Contact Page: If you have any questions, or if you want to know more about the climbing trips, this page provides contact details for the responsible organizers, as well as a contact form to reach out to me.
